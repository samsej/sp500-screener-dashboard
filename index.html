How to read this dashboard
Composite (0–100)
Overall opportunity score combining company quality, trend strength, and risk. Higher is better. Stocks in the AVOID risk tier are scored 0 regardless of other signals.
Quality
Company financial health based on fundamentals. GREAT = strong balance sheet and earnings; GOOD = solid; OK = average; WEAK = concerns present.
Risk
Overall risk tier for the stock. SAFE = low risk; WATCH = elevated volatility or trend weakness; RISKY = high beta or technical breakdown; AVOID = not suitable.
TrendScore (0–100)
Technical momentum score based on price trend, moving averages, and relative strength. Higher means the stock is in a stronger uptrend.
1M %
Price change over the past month. Positive means the stock has risen; negative means it has fallen.
Dist 52W Hi %
How far the current price is below the 52-week high. Higher % means the stock has pulled back more from its recent peak — potentially a larger recovery opportunity, or a sign of weakness.
Days→Earn
Days until the next earnings report. Negative means earnings already passed this cycle. Values near 0 signal imminent earnings — expect higher short-term volatility.
Risk Flags
CLEAR = no flags; HIGH_BETA = moves more than the broad market; BELOW_SMA50 = price under 50-day average; EARNINGS_NEAR = earnings within ~3 weeks.
